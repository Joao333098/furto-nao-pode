Artigos da Confederação - Perguntas e Respostas

Perguntas:

  1. What was one of the reasons the Articles of Confederation created a weak central government?
    Answer: A) They had just fought to be free from a strong central government under the British king.
  2. What power did the Congress under the Articles of Confederation have, according to the video?
    Answer: C) The power to make treaties, maintain an army, and coin money.
  3. How did the Articles of Confederation affect taxes at the federal level?
    Answer: B) Paying federal taxes was voluntary for states, meaning the government couldn’t collect money easily.
  4. What major issue did the government face because it couldn’t collect taxes under the Articles of Confederation?
    Answer: C) The United States couldn’t pay its debts from the Revolutionary War, leading to an economic disaster.
  5. How did Daniel Shays’ Rebellion reveal a weakness in the Articles of Confederation?
    Answer: A) It showed that the national government could not stop the rebellion because it lacked the power to raise an army.
  6. Why was it difficult to make changes to the Articles of Confederation, according to the video?
    Answer: D) Any change needed unanimous approval from all 13 states, making it hard to pass amendments.
  7. How did Shays’ Rebellion and the weaknesses of the Articles of Confederation lead to a new Constitution?
    Answer: B) The weaknesses of the Articles, including the inability to stop the rebellion, led to a call for a stronger central government, resulting in the Constitution.
